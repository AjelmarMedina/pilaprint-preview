# **PILAPRINT SYSTEM PROCESS FLOW** 

## **Website Interface and User Navigation** 

## **FOR STUDENT SIDE AFTER SIGN IN** 

Upon accessing the PILA PRINT website, users are greeted with the logos of participating universities within Dasmariñas, Cavite, including 

- National University – Dasmariñas 

- De La Salle University – Dasmariñas 

- Emilio Aguinaldo College – Cavite 

- National College of Science and Technology (NCST) 

- AISAT College 

- CVSU Dasma 

- Saint Jude Dasma PCU 

- TUP 

- KLD 

- MOL MAGSAYSAY 

- DLSHI 

- FEPC 

1. After selecting their university, the system automatically displays a list of available partner printing shops located near the chosen institution. The printing shops are organized based on proximity and accessibility to provide students with convenient options for their printing needs. 

2. Students can then browse the available printing shops and select their preferred provider based on location, services offered, pricing, or shop ratings. Once a printing shop is chosen, users may upload their files, specify printing requirements, and place their orders directly through the platform. 

This process enables students to efficiently locate nearby printing services, submit printing requests online, and conveniently pick up their completed documents at their selected printing shop without the need to search for available providers manually. 

## **PHASE 1 — USER REGISTRATION & LOGIN** 

### **Student Side** 

Students create an account using: 

- Email Address 

- Mobile Number 

- School/University 

After registration, students gain access to a personal dashboard containing: 

- Location of Nearby University within Dasmarinas, Cavite 

- Active Orders 

- Saved Documents 

- Wallet Balance 

- Rewards Points 

- Transaction History 

- Queue Dashboard Monitoring 

- Terms and COndition 

### **Printing Shop Side** 

Printing shop owners register their businesses by submitting: 

- Business Name 

- Store Location 

- Services Offered 

- Operating Hours 

- Contact Information 

- Pricing Rates 

- BIR / TIN NUMBER ID 

The platform administrator verifies and approves partner shops before activation. Approved businesses gain access to: 

- Shop Dashboard 

- Order Management 

- Sales Analytics 

- Transaction Monitoring 

- Profit Tracking

## **PHASE 6 — GROUP PROJECT COLLABORATION FEATURE** 

For group projects and thesis requirements: 

1. Group leader creates one order. 

2. Members upload files individually. 

3. The system consolidates all files into:

- manual file collection 

- missing files 

- communication delays 

## **PHASE 7 — PAYMENT SYSTEM** 

Students can pay through multiple payment methods including:

- Cash 

- Maya 

- Debit/Credit Cards

### **PRINTING WALLET SYSTEM** 

Students may load credits into a digital Printing Wallet. Benefits include:

## **PHASE 8 — ORDER PROCESSING** 

Once payment is confirmed: 

1. Partner printing shops receive the order through their dashboard. 

2. Orders are categorized into: 

- Pending 

- Ongoing 

- Ready for Pickup 

- Completed 

3. Printing shops process the documents based on customer specifications. 

4. Students receive real-time updates regarding order progress. 

## **PHASE 9 — QUEUE DASHBOARD SYSTEM** 

Once the order is completed, the system automatically generates a Queue Number for claiming. 

Students receive: 

- Queue Number 

- Pickup Notification 

- Estimated Claim Time 

- Shop Details 

Example: 

- “Your order is now ready for pickup. Queue Number: PP-104” 

## **BUSINESS OWNER DASHBOARD SYSTEM** 

Aside from the student dashboard, PilaPrint also includes a dedicated Business Owner Dashboard designed specifically for partner printing shops. This dashboard helps business owners efficiently manage operations, monitor transactions, and track daily business performance in real time. 

The Business Owner Dashboard functions as the operational center of the printing shop within the PilaPrint platform.

- Cancelled Transactions 

Each transaction includes: 

- Order Number 

- Customer Name 

- Service Availed 

- Payment Method
