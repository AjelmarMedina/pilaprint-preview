# PILAPRINT Brand Guidelines (AI Context)

PILAPRINT's branding is built around the Triple P philosophy: **Progress, Performance, and Purpose**. The goal is to maintain a consistent, recognizable identity across all platforms while reflecting the company's mission of providing accessible, reliable, and creative academic support.

## Logo System
- **Primary Logo:** Playful yet professional; rounded typography and soft colors emphasize inclusivity, creativity, and reliability.
- **Wordmark:** Compact version for limited spaces (e.g., email signatures, mobile interfaces).
- **Logo Icon:** Simplified mark for favicons, app icons, and subtle branding applications.
- Designed for scalability, adaptability, and strong brand recognition across digital and print media.

## Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Meaning |

## Typography
- **Fredoka:** Primary font for headlines and key messaging; friendly and approachable.
- **Child Marker:** Secondary font for playful, student-focused communication.
- **Poppins:** Accent font for modern, clean emphasis and supporting text.

## Brand Personality
- Student-centered
- Accessible
- Reliable
- Creative
- Inclusive
- Growth-oriented

## Core Objective
Ensure visual and messaging consistency across all touchpoints while reinforcing PILAPRINT's identity as a transformative academic solutions provider grounded in Progress, Performance, and Purpose.
